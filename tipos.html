Sport PRE: Fuego de Cárdenas

The sport horse seeks functionality and performance in disciplines such as dressage. A legendary example is Fuego de Cárdenas, who demonstrated that the PRE (Purebred Spanish Horse) can compete at the highest Olympic level. These horses tend to be more athletic, with more expansive movements and great impulsion, without losing the essence and noble character of the breed.

Exhibition PRE: Revoltoso

In conformation shows, the focus is on racial purity and adherence to the Baroque standard. Revoltoso is considered one of the purest horses in the world. In these types of horses, the beauty of their conformation is prized: the arched neck, the abundant mane, and the elegant, graceful movements that define the historical aesthetic of the Spanish horse.
