Impressive Morphology

The PRE (Pura Raza Española) is a breed of horse that was officially declared in 1912, but we are certain its origins are much older. A horse that embodies perfect balance and beauty, its medium-sized, slightly arched face, along with its baroque and very rounded body, its abundant mane, and the variety of coat colors make it, for many, the most complete and beautiful horse breed in the world.

Nobility and Temperament

If there is one thing this breed is known for, it is its impressive nobility when around people. They have a calm and friendly character, but always with a great predisposition to work, which makes them a perfect horse for all types of riders.

Elevated Movements

We can certainly say that these horses possess elegant, graceful, and visually beautiful movements. The Spanish horse has an innate ability for collection and elevation, and is also very flexible and versatile, something we can see in its presence in most equestrian disciplines.
