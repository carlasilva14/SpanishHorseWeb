PIAFFÉ

Rhythmic and elevated trot performed in place.

PASSAGE

A very high and rhythmic trot where the horse seems to float.

MOSQUERO

A bristle or leather ornament that hangs from the headstall to shoo away flies

APOYO

Lateral movement where the horse moves sideways crossing its legs.

REUNION

A state where the horse shifts its weight to its hindquarters, gaining lightness.

SERRETA

Metal instrument that is placed on the nose for initial training.
